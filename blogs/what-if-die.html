what happens to someone who dies?

the words below may be triggering, i will not be responsible for any consequences that arise from reading this webpage.


we, as mortal humans, are capable of dying in 1,025,594,402,492,069,688 methods each day. i could wake up and experience a heat stroke. i could die of frostbite. maybe the ozone suddenly breaks open and sunlight sets everything on fire. then i'd die from the carbon monoxide. or perhaps drown from the water from the sprinkler. or perhaps i could wake up with a pig in my bedroom and die of a heartstroke. or perhaps some serial killer comes into my room and kills me. i'll never know.


however, i do know that once i get out of my bed successfully without dying, a doll could charge at me and kill me with that knife i used to cut up onions the day before. or my apartment could crumble into pieces, leaving me under heaps of wood and cement. i mean, metaphorically i am. instead of wood and cement, i am under heaps of an unknown substance called "other people's opinions". and i can't stop thinking about it, just like how i wouldn't escape from the tons of bricks above me.


so, when will i die? from the day i graduate and start working a 9-5 job? from the day i start dating some unknown woman just to please my parents? from the day my child is born and i need to earn money to support my family? or.. from the day i say "i love you" to the woman who deep down doesn't love me back? i don't know. perhaps i'll die on a hospital bed, reminiscing over the moments i had to myself. in preschool. in elementary. in university. everything after that seems to have disappeared from my memori...#$**!!


so, when did i die?

what is dreamcore?

Dreamcore is a surrealist aesthetic that uses motifs commonly associated with dreams, daydreams or nightmares, portrayed through media such as images, videos and, on occasion, music. Dreamcore shares many similarities with surreal memes; however, it does not rely on absurdist humor. Instead, dreamcore focuses on emulating the general feeling of a dream.
